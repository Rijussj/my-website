Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026 · Applies to all RUHI Applications applications

1. Overview

RUHI Applications ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information our apps collect (or rather, do not collect) and how your data is handled.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

None of our apps collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Browsing history or app usage analytics
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Photos, files, or media from your device

3. Local Data Storage

Some apps (like DukanBook) store data you enter directly on your device's local storage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly use an optional backup feature. We have no access to this data.

4. Third-Party Services

Our apps do not integrate any third-party advertising SDKs, analytics platforms, or social media tracking pixels.

5. Permissions

Each app only requests permissions that are strictly necessary for its function. For example, RU Gallery only requests storage access to display your photos. RU Calculator requests zero permissions.

6. Children's Privacy

Our apps are suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any data from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of our apps after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

Questions? Email us at support@myruhi.in
