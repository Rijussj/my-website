RU Browser – Your Privacy Matters

Introduction

RU Browser respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the RU Browser application on Android and Windows.

Information We Collect

RU Browser does not collect personally identifiable information such as name, address, phone number, or precise location.

Basic non-personal technical data (such as app version or crash logs) may be collected only to improve performance and stability.

Permissions

RU Browser requests only the minimum permissions required for essential browser functionality.

We do not access contacts, messages, call logs, or personal files without user action.

Data Usage

Any collected data is used strictly to improve app performance, fix bugs, and enhance user experience.

RU Browser does not sell user data to third parties.

Third-Party Services

RU Browser may use trusted third-party services (such as analytics or crash reporting) that follow their own privacy policies.

These services are used only to improve app quality and reliability.

Children’s Privacy

RU Browser does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.
