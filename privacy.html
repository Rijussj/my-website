Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all applications developed and published under the RU Apps brand, including RU Browser, RU Gallery, RU Calendar and RU Calculator.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and how we keep it safe.

Information Collection

RU Apps do not collect personally identifiable information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Precise location

App-Specific Information

  • RU Browser requires internet access to load websites. Browsing data remains on the user’s device and is not collected by us.
  • RU Calendar may access publicly available system holiday information from online sources to display holiday lists. This does not involve personal user data.
  • RU Gallery and RU Calculator work fully offline and do not require internet access.

Analytics

Some RU Apps may use Firebase Analytics to understand general app usage patterns such as app launches, device type, and performance metrics.

This data is collected in an aggregated and anonymous form and does not identify individual users.

Our apps may also include open-source libraries provided by Google, which do not collect personal user data.

Advertisements

Currently, RU Apps do not display advertisements.

If advertisements are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

Accounts & Login

RU Apps do not require user registration, login, or account creation.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share user data with third parties.

Children’s Privacy

RU Apps are designed to be suitable for a general audience, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect our applications and ensure that user information remains secure.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
