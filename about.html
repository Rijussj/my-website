About RU Apps

RU Apps is an independent software brand focused on building fast, lightweight and privacy-respecting applications for everyday users. Our goal is to deliver clean, reliable and efficient apps that work smoothly even on low-end devices.

What We Build

  • RU Browser – A fast and privacy-focused web browser for Android and Windows.
  • RU Gallery – A simple and lightweight photo gallery app.
  • RU Calendar – A minimal calendar app with essential scheduling features.
  • RU Calculator – A fast and accurate calculator for daily use.

Our Core Values

  • Performance First – Apps optimized for speed and low resource usage.
  • Privacy Focused – Minimal permissions and respect for user data.
  • Simplicity – Clean design without unnecessary complexity.
  • Reliability – Stable apps you can depend on.

Platforms

RU Apps develops applications primarily for:

  • Android
  • Windows

Privacy & Trust

User privacy is a core principle at RU Apps. Our applications avoid unnecessary data collection and request only essential permissions required for functionality.
