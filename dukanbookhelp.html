Getting Started

Basic Setup

Tap the + button on the home screen. Enter the customer's name and phone number. Tap Save. The customer now appears in your list.
Open a customer's profile. Tap "Add Credit". Enter the amount and optional note/date. Tap Save. The balance updates automatically.
Open a customer's profile. Tap "Add Payment". Enter the amount received. The outstanding balance is reduced automatically.

Backup & Reports

Data Management

Go to Settings → Backup & Restore → Create Backup. Save the backup file to your device storage or share to Google Drive.
Go to Settings → Backup & Restore → Restore. Select your backup file. Your data will be restored immediately.
