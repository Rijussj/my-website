DukanBook App Help

This Privacy Policy applies to all applications developed and published under the RU Apps brand, including RU Browser, RU Gallery, RU Calendar and RU Calculator, exclude DukanBook app.

Data Backup

What is Automatic data backup? Your data is automatically backed-up and kept safe so that you never lose your data. In case you changed your phone or wanted to use DukanBook on another device- Simply log in to DukanBook with the same mail as earlier. Enter the Passwood.

App-Specific Information

  • RU Browser requires internet access to load websites. Browsing data remains on the user’s device and is not collected by us.
  • RU Calendar may access publicly available system holiday information from online sources to display holiday lists. This does not involve personal user data.
  • RU Gallery and RU Calculator work fully offline and do not require internet access. For DukanBook App Privacy Policy login https://myruhi.in/dukan-book-privacy-policy

Analytics

Our apps may also include open-source libraries provided by Google, which do not collect personal user data.
