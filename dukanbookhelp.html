DukanBook Help & FAQ

Welcome to the DukanBook Help Center. Here you can find answers to common questions and learn how to use the DukanBook app to manage your shop records.

What is DukanBook?

DukanBook is a simple digital ledger app that helps shopkeepers manage customer credit, track payments, and keep shop records safely in one place.

How do I add a customer?

To add a customer:

  • Open the DukanBook app
  • Tap on Add Customer
  • Enter the customer name and phone number
  • Start adding transactions

How do I record a payment?

  • Open the customer account
  • Tap Add Entry
  • Select Credit or Payment
  • Enter the amount and save

Data Backup

What is Automatic Data Backup?

Your data is automatically backed up and kept safe so that you never lose your shop records. If you change your phone or want to use DukanBook on another device, simply log in with the same email and password to restore your data.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your shop data is securely stored and protected. Only you can access your records using your account credentials.
